Takes

Things I'd argue about

Fatherhood

Present fathers — even imperfect ones — change outcomes. Fatherless homes are one of the roots of crime, stress, and reduced options. More present dads = better society. Not complicated.

Money education

If we taught kids how money works from age 8, we'd have far less debt and far more financially independent adults. Schools skip it entirely. That's a design failure, not an accident.

Consistency

Intensity is exciting but unreliable. Consistency is boring but compounds. The person who shows up daily at 80% beats the person who shows up monthly at 110%. Every domain.

AI in product

AI without revenue logic is theater. Escalation is a feature, not a failure. Evaluation is mandatory. Ship narrow, expand carefully. Most AI features fail the "why would someone pay for this" test.

Accountability

Julia and I came here as immigrants and built a life from scratch. We didn't act like victims. Work hard, follow the rules, stay accountable. That's not just something I believe — it's how we actually got here.

Strength

A strong body is a force multiplier — energy, confidence, resilience, discipline. It bleeds into parenting, work, relationships. Gym isn't vanity. It's maintenance on the machine that runs everything else.
